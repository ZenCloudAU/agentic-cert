- **Duration:** 120 minutes
- **Format:** Proctored · May include interactive components
- **Language:** English
- **Results:** Not immediate for beta — approximately 8 weeks after beta period closes
- **Retake policy:** 24 hours after first attempt; timing varies for subsequent retakes
- **Registration:** Personal MSA account recommended (not work/school AAD — records lost if you leave the organisation)
